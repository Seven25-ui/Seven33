Digital Detox: 24 Hours Without Screens

Imagine a full day without phones, laptops, or TV. At first, it feels strange, maybe even uncomfortable. But soon, you notice clarity, calmness, and more attention to real-world experiences.

A digital detox refreshes your mind and helps you reconnect with people, nature, and yourself. Even if it’s just once a month, this break can reset your habits and improve mental well-being.
